Present Continuous Tense

Definition

🇬🇧 Used to describe actions happening right now or around the current time.

🇹🇷 Şu anda gerçekleşen ya da şu sıralar devam eden eylemleri ifade eder.

🇧🇬 Използва се за действия, които се случват в момента или около настоящето време.

Structure

  • Positive: Subject + am/is/are + Verb-ing
  • Negative: Subject + am/is/are not + Verb-ing
  • Question: Am/Is/Are + Subject + Verb-ing?

Key Words

now, at the moment, right now, currently, still, this week

Example Scenario

🇬🇧 She is watching a movie right now.

🇹🇷 Şu anda film izliyor.

🇧🇬 В момента тя гледа филм.
