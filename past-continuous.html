Past Continuous Tense

Definition

🇬🇧 Used to describe actions that were happening at a specific moment in the past.

🇹🇷 Geçmişte belirli bir anda devam eden eylemleri ifade eder.

🇧🇬 Използва се за действия, които са се случвали в определен момент в миналото.

Structure

  • Positive: Subject + was/were + Verb-ing
  • Negative: Subject + was/were not + Verb-ing
  • Question: Was/Were + Subject + Verb-ing?

Key Words

while, when, at that time, all day, all night, as

Example Scenario

🇬🇧 He was reading a book when I arrived.

🇹🇷 Ben geldiğimde o kitap okuyordu.

🇧🇬 Когато пристигнах, той четеше книга.
