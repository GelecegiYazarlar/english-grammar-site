Future Perfect Tense

Definition

EN: Used to describe actions that will have been completed by a certain time in the future.

TR: Gelecekte belirli bir zamana kadar tamamlanmış olacak eylemleri ifade eder.

BG: Използва се за действия, които ще са приключили до определен момент в бъдещето.

Structure

  • Positive: Subject + will have + V3
  • Negative: Subject + will not have + V3
  • Question: Will + Subject + have + V3?

Key Words

by tomorrow, by next year, by the time, before

Example Scenario

EN: He will have finished the project by tomorrow.

TR: Yarın projeyi bitirmiş olacak.

BG: Той ще е приключил проекта до утре.
