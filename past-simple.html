Past Simple Tense

Definition

EN: Used to express actions that happened at a specific time in the past.

TR: Geçmişte belirli bir zamanda gerçekleşmiş eylemleri ifade eder.

BG: Използва се за действия, случили се в определен момент в миналото.

Structure

  • Positive: Subject + V2
  • Negative: Subject + did not + V1
  • Question: Did + Subject + V1?

Key Words

yesterday, last night, two days ago, in 1990, when I was a child

Example Scenario

EN: I visited my grandparents last weekend.

TR: Geçen hafta sonu büyükannemleri ziyaret ettim.

BG: Миналия уикенд посетих баба и дядо.
