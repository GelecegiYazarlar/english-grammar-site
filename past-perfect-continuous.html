Past Perfect Continuous Tense

Definition

🇬🇧 Describes an action that started in the past and continued up to another point in the past.

🇹🇷 Geçmişte başlayıp geçmişte başka bir noktaya kadar devam etmiş eylemleri ifade eder.

🇧🇬 Описва действие, започнало в миналото и продължило до друг момент в миналото.

Structure

  • Positive: Subject + had been + Verb-ing
  • Negative: Subject + had not been + Verb-ing
  • Question: Had + Subject + been + Verb-ing?

Key Words

for, since, before, until, when, by the time

Example Scenario

🇬🇧 She had been studying for hours when the phone rang.

🇹🇷 Telefon çaldığında saatlerdir ders çalışıyordu.

🇧🇬 Тя учеше с часове, когато телефонът звънна.
