Present Perfect Continuous Tense

Definition

🇬🇧 Describes actions that started in the past and are still continuing or were recently completed.

🇹🇷 Geçmişte başlayıp hâlâ devam eden ya da yakın zamanda sona ermiş eylemleri ifade eder.

🇧🇬 Описва действия, започнали в миналото и продължаващи или току-що приключили.

Structure

  • Positive: Subject + have/has been + Verb-ing
  • Negative: Subject + have/has not been + Verb-ing
  • Question: Have/Has + Subject + been + Verb-ing?

Key Words

since, for, lately, recently, all day, the whole week

Example Scenario

🇬🇧 She has been working all morning.

🇹🇷 Tüm sabah boyunca çalışıyor.

🇧🇬 Тя работи цяла сутрин.
