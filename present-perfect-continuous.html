Present Perfect Continuous Tense

Definition

EN: Describes actions that started in the past and are still continuing or were recently completed.

TR: Geçmişte başlayıp hâlâ devam eden ya da yakın zamanda sona ermiş eylemleri ifade eder.

BG: Описва действия, започнали в миналото и продължаващи или току-що приключили.

Structure

  • Positive: Subject + have/has been + Verb-ing
  • Negative: Subject + have/has not been + Verb-ing
  • Question: Have/Has + Subject + been + Verb-ing?

Key Words

since, for, lately, recently, all day, the whole week

Example Scenario

EN: She has been working all morning.

TR: Tüm sabah boyunca çalışıyor.

BG: Тя работи цяла сутрин.
