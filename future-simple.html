Future Simple Tense

Definition

EN: Used to express actions that will happen in the future.

TR: Gelecekte gerçekleşecek eylemleri ifade eder.

BG: Използва се за действия, които ще се случат в бъдеще.

Structure

  • Positive: Subject + will + Verb
  • Negative: Subject + will not + Verb
  • Question: Will + Subject + Verb?

Key Words

tomorrow, next week, in a year, soon, later, one day

Example Scenario

EN: They will travel to Spain next summer.

TR: Gelecek yaz İspanya'ya seyahat edecekler.

BG: Те ще пътуват до Испания следващото лято.
