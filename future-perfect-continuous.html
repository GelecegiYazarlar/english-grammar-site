Future Perfect Continuous Tense

Definition

🇬🇧 Describes actions that will have been happening up to a point in the future.

🇹🇷 Gelecekte belirli bir zamana kadar devam etmekte olacak eylemleri ifade eder.

🇧🇬 Описва действия, които ще са се случвали до определен момент в бъдещето.

Structure

  • Positive: Subject + will have been + Verb-ing
  • Negative: Subject + will not have been + Verb-ing
  • Question: Will + Subject + have been + Verb-ing?

Key Words

by next year, for five years, by the time, in a few months

Example Scenario

🇬🇧 By 2025, she will have been living in Paris for 10 years.

🇹🇷 2025 yılına kadar 10 yıldır Paris'te yaşıyor olacak.

🇧🇬 До 2025 тя ще е живяла в Париж от 10 години.
