Present Simple Tense

Definition

EN: Used to describe habitual actions, general truths, and fixed arrangements.

TR: Alışkanlıklar, genel gerçekler ve sabit düzenlemeleri ifade eder.

BG: Използва се за навици, общи истини и фиксирани уговорки.

Structure

  • Positive: Subject + Verb (he/she/it + Verb+s)
  • Negative: Subject + do/does not + Verb
  • Question: Do/Does + Subject + Verb?

Key Words

always, usually, often, never, every day, sometimes, rarely

Example Scenario

EN: He drinks coffee every morning.

TR: Her sabah kahve içer.

BG: Той пие кафе всяка сутрин.
