Present Simple Tense

Definition

🇬🇧 Used to describe habitual actions, general truths, and fixed arrangements.

🇹🇷 Alışkanlıklar, genel gerçekler ve sabit düzenlemeleri ifade eder.

🇧🇬 Използва се за навици, общи истини и фиксирани уговорки.

Structure

  • Positive: Subject + Verb (he/she/it + Verb+s)
  • Negative: Subject + do/does not + Verb
  • Question: Do/Does + Subject + Verb?

Key Words

always, usually, often, never, every day, sometimes, rarely

Example Scenario

🇬🇧 He drinks coffee every morning.

🇹🇷 Her sabah kahve içer.

🇧🇬 Той пие кафе всяка сутрин.
