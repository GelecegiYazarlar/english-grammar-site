Present Perfect Tense

Definition

EN: Used to describe actions that occurred at an unspecified time before now. The exact time is not important.

TR: Belirsiz bir zamanda gerçekleşmiş olayları ifade eder. Zaman önemli değildir.

BG: Използва се за действия, случили се в неопределен момент преди настоящето.

Structure

  • Positive: Subject + have/has + V3
  • Negative: Subject + have/has not + V3
  • Question: Have/Has + Subject + V3?

Key Words

already, yet, ever, never, just, so far, recently, lately, since, for

Example Scenario

EN: She has already finished her homework.

TR: Ödevini çoktan bitirdi.

BG: Тя вече е свършила домашната си работа.
